We Believe

  • In the Triune God - Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • In God who is the creator of all things.
  • In Jesus Christ as the Son of God, His virgin birth, sinless life, atoning death, and resurrection.
  • That salvation is by grace through faith, not by works.
  • In the indwelling presence and work of the Holy Spirit in every believer.
  • In the second coming of Jesus Christ to gather His church.
  • In the authority of the Holy Scriptures as God’s word for life and conduct.
  • In water baptism and the Lord’s Supper as ordinances of the church.
