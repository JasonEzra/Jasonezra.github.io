About Our Ministry

Rev. Ezra Venkatesh started a small church with only two members in the year 2003 at Rajapalayam. By the grace of God, gradually the church started growing.

As Rev. Ezra preached the word of faith, God made him understand the gospel of grace. He began to preach the unconditional love and favour of God.
